---------------------------------------------------------------------------- About My Film --------------------------------------------------------------------------

I created a movie composed of various clips showcasing the Nyuad campus. The film's primary emphasis lies on capturing the captivating sounds that encompass the campus environment; birds chirping, wind rustling through trees, cars passing by, and the bustling presence of people, among others.
